Projects

In my ongoing research, I focus on the bioremediation of heavy metals, a critical area in environmental biotechnology. My project aims to develop and optimize biological methods to remove and neutralize heavy metal contaminants from the environment. Utilizing advanced biotechnology tools and techniques, I investigate the efficiency of various microorganisms and plants in accumulating and detoxifying heavy metals. This research not only addresses environmental pollution but also explores sustainable solutions for restoring ecosystems affected by industrial activities. Through this project, I strive to contribute to cleaner and safer environments by leveraging the natural capabilities of biological systems.
